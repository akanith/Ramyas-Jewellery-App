| Neutral 900 | `#171717` | Primary text |

## Usage Notes

- Always use Gold Primary on dark backgrounds for maximum contrast
- Never use pure black (#000000) — use Charcoal instead
- Maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for all text
